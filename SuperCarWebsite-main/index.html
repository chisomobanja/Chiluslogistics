Introduction and Company Background

Based in the capital city of Zambia, Chilus Logistics and Enterprises offers its clients a wide range of transportation services throughout Zambia. Since the beginning of 2023, we have been strongly committed to providing our clients with the highest level of service in the car hire business sector. Building and maintaining relationships with our customers is at the heart of our business; a business that believes in and utilises a planning and management style that is consultative and systematic. We believe in a proactive approach to all aspects of our business, but in particular to quality, safety, and delivery. By getting this combination right, we give our customers confidence and peace of mind during the process of planning, travel, and handover of the vehicles. The hands-on responsibilities and team approach of business directors Madalitso K. Banja, Racheal Mfune, and Chisomo Y. Banja underpin our organization with a powerful and proven synergy. We rely on some of the most experienced drivers in the industry. For this reason, we are better able to maintain high standards of quality, safety, and delivery. We are committed to the future of our industry. Our business offers quality car hire services with a wide array of comfortable cars as shown below under the subsection ‘Our fleet’.
